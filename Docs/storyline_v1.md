# Storyline Framework · 剧情框架
## Crown: The Gilded Cage · 王权

---

## Act I · 第一幕：蜜月期 The Honeymoon (Rounds 1–4)

> 局势表面平静，各方势力试探新国王的底线。玩家开始摸索规则，尚不知危险已至。
> The surface is calm. The factions are testing the new King. The player is learning the rules, unaware of the danger ahead.

---

### Round 1 · 第一轮

| | English | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| **NPC** | Minister | 大臣 |
| **Plot Goal** | Drain Treasury early | 提前掏空国库 |
| **Surface Request** | "The coronation banquet expenses are overdue." | "陛下，登基宴会的账单已经逾期。" |
| **Hidden Motive** | Empty the King's pockets before he can fund a private guard. | 在国王组建私人卫队之前掏空他的钱袋。 |
| **Key Resource** | Treasury | 金库 |
| **Uncle's Attitude** | Amused — mocking your 'expensive' taste. | 戏谑——嘲笑陛下"奢靡"的品味。 |

## Act II · 第二幕：暗流涌动 The Undercurrent (Rounds 5–8)

> 各方势力开始主动施压，诉求变得强硬。未婚妻出现，带来唯一的真实情报。舅舅不再掩饰。
> The factions begin to push harder. The Princess appears with the only real intelligence. The Uncle stops pretending.

## Act III · 第三幕：危机爆发 The Breaking Point (Rounds 9–12)

> 四面楚歌。多方同时施压，任何一个资源都岌岌可危。舅舅的政变进入倒计时。
> Enemies on all sides. Multiple factions press simultaneously. Every resource is at risk. The coup is imminent.

---

### Round 9 · 第九轮

| | English | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| **NPC** | Minister + Priest | 大臣 + 教士 |
| **Plot Goal** | Combined Assault | 联合夹击 |
| **Surface Request** | "The Church demands the unpaid taxes from the empty Treasury!" | "教会要求从空空如也的国库中补缴拖欠的税款！" |
| **Hidden Motive** | Force a bankruptcy-driven conflict between the Crown and the Church. | 强行制造王室与教会之间由破产引发的冲突。 |
| **Key Resource** | Treasury / Church | 金库 / 教会 |

## Ending Conditions · 结局条件

| Ending · 结局 | Trigger · 触发条件 |
|---|---|
| **Resource Death** · 资源死亡 | Any resource reaches 0 or 100 before Round 12 |
| **Regent's Coup** · 舅舅政变 | Uncle triggers coup based on player behavior |
| **Abdication** · 退位 | Player accepts exile in Round 12 |
| **Civil War** · 内战 | Player refuses and triggers uprising in Round 12 |
| **Survival** · 生存 | Player reaches Round 12 with all resources in 20–80 range |
